Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

ResuCraft ("we", "our", or "us") provides tools to create, edit, and export resumes. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information (registered users): username, email address, and hashed password.
  • User Content: resume data you enter or save (such as education, experience, and projects).
  • Uploaded Resume Data: if you upload a resume PDF for parsing, text/content may be processed to structure your resume fields.
  • Feedback Data: message content and optional email if you submit feedback.
  • Usage Metrics: limited aggregate metrics (for example, total downloads), not intended to identify individual users.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide resume creation, editing, and export features.
  • To authenticate users and secure saved data.
  • To process uploaded resumes and auto-fill fields.
  • To improve product quality and fix issues.

3. Guest Mode

Guest users can use core resume-building features without creating an account. In guest mode, resume data is not persisted in our database unless explicitly saved through a registered account workflow.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as AI processing providers) to parse uploaded resume content and enable app functionality. These services process data only for requested features.

5. Data Security

  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS.
  • Passwords are stored as hashed values (not plain text).

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Registered users can delete saved resumes from within the app. If you need additional data-related support, contact us using the details below.

7. Children’s Privacy

ResuCraft is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised date.
