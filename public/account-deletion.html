Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of their ResuCraft account and associated data.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Log in to your ResuCraft account.
  2. Open the Dashboard.
  3. Use the Delete Account option.
  4. Confirm the deletion prompt.
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

What data is deleted

  • Your account profile (username, email, auth record).
  • Your saved resume/CV documents associated with your account.

What data may be retained

We may retain limited technical logs for security, abuse prevention, and legal compliance for a short period, after which they are removed according to our internal retention practices.

Deleting some data without deleting account

Users can delete saved CVs individually from the dashboard without deleting the entire account.
